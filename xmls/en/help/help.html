Navigation Instructions:

Move ahead one page.

Move back one page.

Pause the audio, animation, or video.

Play the audio, animation, or video.

Restart the page from the beginning.

Save your location in the course and exit.

Course Review:
Use the drop down menu to jump to a specific page.
Usually, you can only jump to previously viewed pages.

Progress Bar:
The Progress Bar shows your current location in the course.
Hover over it to see the current page number and title.

Learning Mode:
To change your learning mode, click this icon and select your desired mode, such as video, audio, or text. Some modes will require more or less bandwidth. For example, Video-High Bandwith versus Video-Low Bandwidth.

Auto-Forward:
Auto-Forward automatically advances you to the next slide once the current slide is complete. You can turn this feature on or off with this button.

Glossary and Resource Center:
Displays the glossary and / or resource center.
